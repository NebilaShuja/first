ASIA:THE LAND OF DIVERSITIES
  • Largest continent in the world
  • The continent that has one third of the total land area
  • The continent with highest populationAbout 60% of world's population lives in Asia

A continent of diversities

The title continent of diversities is suitable for Asia because Asia is a continent marked by diverse geographical charactors.The HIMALAYA, the highest mountain range in the world situates in Asia. The regions which lies below sea level also situates in this continent. The region which receives maximum rainfall situates in this continent. There are also places in Asia which receives less rainfall. It experiences high temperature in the region close to the equator and very low temperature near the polar regions. It is also a continent with diverse distribution of population. We can see the regions with high density of population, moderate population and regions with sparse population on the same continent.
